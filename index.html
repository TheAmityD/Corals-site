And our short story~

Hello! We're Rynn and Joan! We're typical students who love to play Roblox. And here's how we got here:

5 years ago, a friend of ours suggested us to install Roblox. Rynn was hesitant. Our mother agreed, but said that we had wait until August. Joan was so exited, months passed like minutes and August soon arrived. But our mother rejected us. We were good kids, so we obeyed. A year later, we couldn't wait any longer. Joan secretly signed up and we both ended up here :)
